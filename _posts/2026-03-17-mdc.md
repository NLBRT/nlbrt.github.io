## Debian? More like lesb- : The journey to and from, and my experience at MiniDebConf 2026

It has been a while since I've wrote about anything here (or even attending an event outside of the ones I organise in general), so here goes something!

MiniDebConf is a local version of DebConf that focuses on Debian as free and open-source software by conducting talks and workshops. Debian is an operating system build by a community of people. It is a freedom-respecting operating system which gives users freedom.

The folks in the Indian Debian community has been organising the MiniDebConf in the country the past few years now, with this year which happened in Kanpur, specifically at the Indian Institute of Technology at Kanpur.
